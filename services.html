Our Services

At Shamba La Nyama, we offer a range of services to meet your meat needs. From home delivery to restaurant supply, we are committed to providing fresh and high-quality products to our customers in Dodoma.

🏠 Home Delivery

We offer convenient home delivery of fresh meat products, ensuring you receive your order quickly and safely.

🍽 Restaurant Supply

We supply fresh meat to restaurants and catering businesses, ensuring they have the best quality products for their customers.

🎉 Bulk Orders

We cater to large orders for events, parties and special occasions, ensuring you have enough fresh meat for your guests.

🐐 Live Animal Sales

We offer live goats and chickens for sale, perfect for special occasions and cultural events.

📞 Customer Support

Our friendly team is available to answer your questions, take orders and provide assistance.
